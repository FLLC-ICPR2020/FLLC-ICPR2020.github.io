2nd Grand Challenge of 106-Point Facial Landmark Localization

ICPR 2020, Milan,Italy,13 Sep,2020

Home Datasets Evaluation Leaderboard

Training data

The dataset is an incremental of JD-landmark [1-7], consisting of about 20,000 images with 106-point landmark annotation. The images and landmarks will be released on March 30, 2020.

Validation data

It consists of 2,000 images, covering large variations of pose, expression and occlusion, are selected from open source web face dataset [8]. The images will be released on April 7, 2020.

Test data

It contains 2,000 web face images as well. To prevent cheating on the test set, the test images will be released on June 24, 2020 [00:00 a.m. UTC+1], and the submission deadline is June 25, 2020 [00:00 a.m. UTC+1]. Participants will have 24 hours to evaluate on the test set.
